Personal Prevention & Protection

Regardless of public health orders, the guidance to protect yourself and your loved ones remains the same. Get vaccinated. It’s one of the best ways to protect yourself and help end the pandemic.

Avoid large gatherings or crowds, especially indoors, until the risk of COVID-19 transmission is lower. Check the state’s guidance by sector for additional recommendations for events and gatherings.

Remember the Big Five Rules for safety

* Outdoor gatherings are safer than indoor gatherings. * Online gatherings are safest, especially for people at high risk of severe illness. * Keep 6 feet of distance from people who don’t live with you and have not been fully vaccinated. * Wear a mask over your nose and mouth when in public, especially indoors. * Wash your hands frequently, or use hand sanitizer if soap and water are not available.
